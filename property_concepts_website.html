Payments & Quotes

Quotes are provided before work begins. Pricing depends on the job size, location, materials required and the type of service requested.

Payment may be requested by invoice, bank transfer, card payment or another agreed method. For larger jobs, a deposit or progress payment may be required before work starts or as work progresses.

Typical payment process

  • Client makes an enquiry
  • We discuss the job and inspect if required
  • A quote or estimate is provided
  • Client approves the work
  • Payment is made by invoice or agreed payment method

Customer Policies

The following information is provided to make our service terms clear for customers and payment providers.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

If a customer needs to cancel or reschedule work, please contact us as soon as possible. Refunds for deposits or pre-paid work are assessed based on the timing of cancellation, materials already purchased, and work already completed.

Where work has already been completed, payment is required for the completed work. If there is an issue with the service, customers should contact us promptly so we can review and attempt to resolve the matter fairly.

Service Delivery Policy

Service timeframes are agreed directly with the customer and depend on job size, weather, material availability and site access. We aim to communicate clearly if a delay occurs.

Privacy Policy

We collect customer contact details and job information only for the purpose of quoting, scheduling, completing work, invoicing and communicating with customers. We do not sell customer information. Payment information is handled by the relevant payment processor and is not stored by us directly.

Terms of Service

By approving a quote or booking work, the customer agrees to provide safe and reasonable access to the property and to pay for approved work as agreed. Any additional work outside the original quote may require approval and may incur extra charges.
